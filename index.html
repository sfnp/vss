Vidhya Sanskar School.

Vidhya Sanskar School established in the year 2005, has schooling program from Nursery to Grade 12 in the name of Vidhya Sanskar School. It also runs prestigious Cambridge GCE A-level program. This school is a distinct school, valued by recognized and prominent academicians, also respected by our competitors.
